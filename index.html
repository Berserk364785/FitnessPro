❓ How to use

Frequently asked questions
📷 Why does the app need camera access?
The camera is used only locally on your device — the app analyses your body position using the MediaPipe AI model (Google) directly in the browser. No video is sent or stored.
📐 How should I position myself in front of the camera?
For most exercises (push-ups, squats, lunges) stand sideways to the camera 1.5–2.5 m away so your whole body — head to feet — is in frame. Tap 🔊 next to the hint to hear the exact recommendation for the selected exercise.
🔢 Why isn't my rep being counted?
Usually it's because the range of motion isn't full enough, lighting is poor, or part of your body is out of frame. Watch the angle indicator in the top-right of the video — reps are counted based on that number.
⚡ What are XP and levels?
Every rep earns you experience (XP). Accumulate enough and you level up. At higher levels, XP per rep decreases — mix up your exercises to progress faster.
🏆 How does the online leaderboard work?
Your result (name, level, XP) is published to the shared table when you finish a workout. Your photo avatar stays visible only to you — an emoji is shown in the leaderboard for privacy.
🎥 Can I upload my own video instead of using the camera?
This feature is still in development — only Camera mode is available for now, and it's fully functional. We'll announce video mode separately when it's ready.
